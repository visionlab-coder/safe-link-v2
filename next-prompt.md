A screen for the foreign worker to read and sign off on a Daily Safety Briefing (TBM).

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- **Theme**: Dark Mode
- **Colors**: Deep grey/black backgrounds. Bright red for alerts/TBM.
- **Style**: Glassmorphism (subtle borders, translucent backgrounds like `bg-slate-800/80`, outer glows).
- **Usability**: Extremely large touch targets, high contrast, large fonts for outdoor visibility.
- **Layout**: Mobile-first, stacked vertical layouts with distinct, separated cards.

**Page Structure:**
1. Header: "SAFE-LINK Worker Mode"
2. TBM Content Card: 
   - A red alert border. 
   - Original Text (e.g. in Korean) in smaller font.
   - Translated Text (e.g. in Vietnamese or Thai) in extremely large, bold text.
   - A button to "Listen via Voice (TTS)".
3. Signature Area:
   - A large boxed area saying "Please sign here"
   - A giant green "Confirm & Sign" button at the bottom.
